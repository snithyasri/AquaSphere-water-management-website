Frequently Asked Questions

Is Aquasphere free to use?

Yes! The platform is completely free for all citizens and volunteers.

How quickly are reports addressed?

Turnaround time varies by issue severity, but critical alerts are typically triaged within 2 hours.

Can I remain anonymous?

Absolutely. You can choose to submit reports anonymously, though creating an account helps track progress.
